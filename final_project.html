The Twitter YIMBY comes from a variety of political backgrounds. As seen below, in the case of Adam Zivo, one of these backgrounds is an "eclectic" set of politics. Zivo is an opinion writer for conservative paper National Post. Despite his conservative background, Zivo is a self-identified YIMBY: he goes so far as to identify as such while embedded in Ukraine covering the Russian invasion.

The YIMBY in me, as we pass through the suburbs of Lviv, Ukraine: “Look at all these triplexes.”

— Adam Zivo (@ZivoAdam) March 10, 2022

Opposition to YIMBYism also stems from a variety of political backgrounds. See below for a tweet from a socialist Twitter user who disparages the notion of a socialist YIMBY:

being a socialist who is also a yimby is like being a vegetarian who also eats meat. you can’t be a socialist that believes the free market will solve our housing crisis.
